History

We were born in the heart of the wilderness, where water and adventure intertwine, and that is where our passion for rafting was born. Founded in 2020, our company has been dedicated to offering unforgettable experiences to those who love excitement and natural beauty.

Since our inception, we have explored the most challenging rivers and breathtaking landscapes, guided by a team of passionate and highly trained experts. Our mission is to share the thrill of rafting in a safe and professional manner, creating lasting memories for each of our adventurer.
